Rules:

      Each player begins with 5 of each weapon(rock, paper, scissor)
      Click on their respective images to play your weapon
      The computer will be picking a random weapon
      Both players can't pick a weapon that has a supply of 0
      If you win your round, you keep your weapon and the computer's weapon is added to your supply
      If the computer wins the round, it keeps their weapon and it gets your weapon added to its supply
      If either player is to play the same weapon 3 times in a row, that weapon will not be counted towards the round and will be removed from the game
      There is to the right of this rules page that tracks each round
      Once the game ends, there will be a message at the top that says who won and who lost
      There is a reset button to reset the game.
